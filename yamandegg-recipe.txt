Ingredients:
- Yam
- 2 fresh eggs
- 1 large tomato
- 1/2 onion
- 2 small scotch bottoms
- Oil for frying

1. Peel and slice the yam, then wash thoroughly
2. Boil with salt to taste
3. Beat eggs and add salt to taste
4. Heat oil in frying pan, then add chopped onion and scotch bottoms
5. Once onions are transparent, add chopped tomato and simmer for a few minutes
6. Add eggs and stir until done
7. Serve yams and allow to cool for a few minutes, then add eggs from pan.
Done!
